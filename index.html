FAQ

Questions we get
all the time

Is SahiDegree completely free for students?
Yes, 100% free for students. Getting matched, browsing programs, comparing colleges, and receiving your AI recommendations — all free. We earn from colleges who want to list premium programs and from optional application services you can choose.
Are all programs UGC-DEB approved?
Yes. We only list programs from universities with valid UGC-DEB (Distance Education Bureau) recognition for online programs. We verify each college's status directly from the UGC website and update it every quarter.
How accurate is the AI matching?
Our matching engine analyzes 5 dimensions: Program Fit, College Trust, ROI, Career Switch Feasibility, and your personal preferences. In user surveys, 94% of students said their top match felt "accurate" or "very accurate" for their situation. The AI also tells you WHY it matched each program — no black box.
Will colleges contact me without my permission?
Never. Your profile is private by default. If you choose to "Apply" or "Express Interest" in a program, only then is your information shared with that specific college. We do not sell leads to colleges.
What is the salary data based on?
Salary estimates come from a combination of college-reported placement data, LinkedIn public profile data for alumni, and government wage data from the National Career Service Portal. We clearly mark the confidence level and data source for each salary figure.
Can I pay college fees through SahiDegree?
Yes. For partner colleges, you can download the official admission form and pay fees directly through our secure Razorpay-powered payment system. Payments are processed in INR, support UPI, cards, and net banking.
